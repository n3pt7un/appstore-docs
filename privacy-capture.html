Capture — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Capture is a local-first application. All content you save — captures, links, images, voice notes, transcripts, search indexes, and AI-generated tags — is stored exclusively on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Capture does not have a backend. We do not operate any servers that receive your data.

Analytics & tracking

None. No third-party analytics, advertising, or telemetry SDKs are present in the app.

Permissions

Capture may request access to your microphone and speech recognition. These are used solely on-device to record and transcribe voice notes. No audio leaves your device.

Crash reports

If you opt in to Apple's TestFlight or App Store crash sharing, anonymized crash logs are sent to Apple under their terms. We do not receive personally identifiable information.

Children

Capture is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
